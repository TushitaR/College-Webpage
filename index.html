About Us

Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT), established in 2001 and affiliated with VTU, Belagavi, is renowned for its high standards in quality and commitment. DBIT offers UG (BE) programs in various engineering disciplines, including Computer Science, Information Science, Electronics and Communication, Electrical and Electronics, Telecommunication, Mechanical, and Civil Engineering, along with an MBA program.

Faculty: Highly qualified and experienced faculty members mentor students towards academic excellence and up-to-date industry knowledge. They continuously enhance their skills and qualifications.

Infrastructure: DBIT boasts state-of-the-art facilities such as Smart Classrooms, Well-equipped Laboratories, Skill Development Centre, PLM Training Centre, Kuka Robotics, and several Incubation Centres, ensuring a superior learning experience.

Industry Interaction: DBIT promotes industry readiness through training programs, guest lectures, and personality development initiatives. The institution supports research and innovative activities, encouraging staff and students to develop their ideas.

Placement Cell: A dedicated Training and Placement Cell facilitates student recruitment and provides career guidance, with many top companies recruiting DBIT graduates.
